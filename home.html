VLSI Design & Embedded Systems

Very-large-scale integration (VLSI) is the way of creating an integrated circuit (IC) by combining millions of transistors into a single chip which is based on miniaturization of microchip used in present day electronic hardwae, medical instrument etc. Design and realization of VLSI systems using next generation VLSI technologies require alliance between scientists and engineers in the areas of system architecture, logic/circuit design, chip/wafer fabrication, packaging, testing etc.In this Generation of noble design, specifications and verification are performed at all levels of abstraction which includes system, register-transfer, logic, circuit, transistor, and process levels.
