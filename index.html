School of Computer and Systems Sciences


School of Computer and Systems Sciences was established in the year 1974 as one of the premier teaching centers of Computer Science discipline in the country. School offers MCA (6 Sem), M.Tech (4 Sem) and PhD programs.

Who all should attend

The participants would be mostly professionals namely Engineers, Researchers and Academicians/Students, including IEEE members and India Electronics and Semiconductor Association (IESA) members. IEEE Delhi Chapter shall be supporting our event.
